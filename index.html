Who We Are

Two names. One standard of quality.

Sam & Ivan The Chimney Guys is a family-run, locally owned business based out of Lowell and Methuen, proudly serving Massachusetts and New Hampshire. With over a decade of hands-on experience each, we combined our skills to deliver the best service experience possible for every homeowner we work with.

Our expertise is rooted in training with the Chimney Safety Institute of America (CSIA). We treat your home like our own, and we value the relationships we build with our clients as much as the work itself.

  • CSIA-certified chimney sweeps
  • Fully insured & all work guaranteed
  • Honest pricing & free estimates
